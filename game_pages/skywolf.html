Summary

2D vehicle action-adventure side scroller where you drive your flying vessel through the skies. Use the different stations on your ship to protect it against obstacles and enemies.

Project Details

Made as a one-year class project with a team of 5 artists and 5 programmers. The engine was done by us (ctrl+F5 for editor mode).

My Contribution

I was very involved in the engine development. With Iñaki I did the multithreaded resource loading. I was very involved in the design throughout the whole project. I also did some gameplay programming like the flamethrower and the reloader. I refactored the station system.
